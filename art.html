ART

Digital Art has become my main hobby that I work on every chance I get.
Here is a brief timeline of my journey through art

2015

Present


These have all been drawn recently using the program Krita.
I learned a lot of new techniques over the years and hope to improve more in the years to come!

Early 2015


This is when I started to get more serious about digital painting.
I continued doing daily sketches and creating more original concepts.

2014

Late 2014


I began my daily sketches in late 2014. I hoped to improve as much as possible.
I started doing a lot of photo studies to try to improve my anatomy and technique.

Summer 2014


My first time trying digital painting. It was a rough start, but I enjoyed every moment of it.
The hardest part was learning what programs and brushes would work best for me.

2007-2013

Traditional Art Years


I've taken art classes as electives in middle and high school.
When I first started all my figures were rectangular and my scenes had horrible compositions.
But my classes slowly taught me how to improve and brought me where I am today.
