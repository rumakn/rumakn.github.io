Natalie Rumak

Content Artist | Programmer

Hi, I'm Natalie! I'm currently studying Digital Arts and Sciences at the University of Florida. I also work as a content artist for Shiny Bolt Games where I create models, textures, and concept art for our current project, Prisma.

Skills and Interests

3D Modeling - I was first drawn to the challenges and rewards of the 3D modeling process. I loved learning how to bring form to ideas and concepts as models and giving each work personality. I also enjoy texturing, rigging and animating the characters I create. Working as an intern and then content artist allowed me to improve quickly and develop a love for creating game art.

Digital Painting - In 2014 I decided that I wanted to learn digital art. I already had a background in traditional painting and sculpture work, but was surprised by how different it was. I fell in love with digital painting quickly and have practiced it as often as possible since then. Learning digital art also gave me experience using Photoshop, Krita, and Illustrator.

Programming - My love for math and puzzles led me to take my first porgramming class. As soon as I saw my lines of code transform into a fractal, game, and adventure I was hooked. Since then I worked on front end development as part of a web dev team, created a stick figure animation program, and recreated the game "Unblock Me". These projects gave me experience using HTML, CSS, JS, MEAN.JS, Java (including Java's Swing GUI), C++, and OpenGL Utility Toolkit.
