About Me

Hi, I'm Natalie! I love creating interactive, creative, and human-centered products.

I currently work at Brex as a Software Engineer II on the website design systems team. In this role I have supported product launches by building new interactive components and extending our modules in our CMS to enhance our design language. My focus is supporting other teams in being able to update the site quickly and to a high standard.

I previously worked at TDSynnex as a Senior Digital Marketing Specialist / Web Developer, with a focus in developing interactive web experiences for clients like IBM, HPE, Microsoft Surface, and Dell Technologies. In this role, I often had the opportunity to explore new creative ideas through rapid prototyping and brainstorming with the design team. It gave me the chance to be a consultant for tech limitations, but also allowed me to give creative input from project start to finish.

I also previously worked in game development as an environment artist at Shiny Bolt, LLC. From painting full illustrations, to 3D modeling and animating props for the game, this role let me fully explore my creative skills and gave me experience working with some interesting programs like Maya, Zbrush, and the Unity Game Engine!

No matter what I do, I love learning new skills and applying both technology and creativity to my work.
